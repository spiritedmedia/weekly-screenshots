Denver weather: Warm and dry until fall arrives on Friday

Andrew Kenney Follow
Posted on September 20, 20177:01 am Categories Denver news

Good morning. The high in Denver today is 82 degrees, and skies should be clear, according to the National Weather Service. Temperatures should be warmer tomorrow before a cold front moves into the area.

On Friday, the first day of fall, high temperatures will begin to decline. It will be 79 degrees on Friday, 67 on Saturday and 59 on Sunday, with chances of precipitation all weekend, according to NWS.
